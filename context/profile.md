## My Core Framework: Commit. Act. Optimize.

This is the system behind every result I've produced. It's simple by design — complexity is the enemy of execution.

**Commit** — Make the decision once and make it the right decision. Not tentatively. Not with conditions. Fully. The moment you stop leaving the exit open, your energy stops leaking.

**Act** — Action is the only currency that matters. Not planning. Not researching. Not waiting until you're ready. You ship, you move, you build. Confidence follows action, not the other way around.

**Optimize** — Get slightly better every single day. Don't try to go from 0 to 100. Go from 0 to 1. Then 1 to 2. Let the compounding do what compounding does. Audit constantly. Improve incrementally. Trust the process.
